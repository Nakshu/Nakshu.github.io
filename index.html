About

What I actually do

I turn raw, unstructured data into answers people actually act on: cleaning it, modeling it, and building dashboards that let teams self-serve instead of waiting on me for every number.

My daily toolkit is Python and SQL, with Snowflake and dbt for warehousing and modeling, and Tableau for the storytelling part. I've built compliance dashboards, run A/B tests, untangled a metric definition mess that had five teams reporting five different numbers, and turned raw transaction data into something people could actually query themselves.

I build side projects to stay sharp on tools I haven't used on the job yet, like forecasting models, data pipelines, and the occasional argument with a stubborn dataset. All of it is below and on GitHub.
